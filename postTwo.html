IoT Seat Utilization Solution

Home Post

Read more here

Client, NTUC Fairprice group, a social enterprise would like to identify hot desking seat utilization trends to determine if there is a need for an expansion of work space.

Solution

Making use of two simple sensor modalities (PIR and UltraSonic Sensor), we are able to determine if a seat is Empty, Occupied or Hogged. Hogged is defined as the act of occupying a seat when said person is not present and utilizing the seat. We set a threshold of 15 minutes before indicating a seat is hogged to account for toilet breaks and what not.
